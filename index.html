  • Mission

    1. I want to read. Understand. Know. And repeat.

    2. I want to discover my own self. I want to meditate. Feel my breath. Chase my mind as it runs a million thoughts. Practice the maniacal process of controlling them.

    3. I want to know people. I want to think about what makes them, they. I want to learn about what makes me different and if any better.

    4. I want to learn the art of dodging bullshit. I want to practice being the calm in a chaos and the silence in a shock.

    5. I want to solve my problems. I want to face them head on and let them scatter me into pieces. I then want to pick myself up and work like a madman to clean up the mess.
